FAQ

Questions answered

What platforms does PostPilot support? +
Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn and X (Twitter). Each platform gets content optimised specifically for its format, audience and algorithm — not just the same post duplicated.
Which languages are available? +
English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese and Dutch. Content is written natively for each language — not translated. Arabic and Japanese are coming soon.
Do I need any social media or marketing experience? +
Absolutely not. If you can copy and paste, you can use PostPilot. Fill in your business details, hit generate, copy the posts and paste them into your social media apps. That's it.
Will the content sound like my business? +
Yes. You choose your brand tone (friendly, bold, luxe, professional, etc.) and the AI tailors everything to match. The content references your specific business name, location and any special details you add.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT? +
ChatGPT is a general tool — you'd have to write your own prompts, figure out the right format for each platform, add hashtags manually, and start from scratch every time. PostPilot is built specifically for social media content — it knows the right format, character limits, hashtag strategy and best practices for every platform automatically. It saves you hours.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — no contracts, no commitments. Cancel with one click and you won't be charged again. We're confident you'll stay because PostPilot genuinely saves you time every week.
